# 001 — Platform: Shopify Basic

**Decision:** Build the store on Shopify Basic ($29/mo annual) with Shopify Payments.

**Alternatives considered:**
- Square Online free tier — $0/mo but 3.3% + 30¢ online (raised Jan 2026), cannot password-protect shop pages, no back-in-stock alerts, preorders need Plus ($49/mo).
- Wix / Squarespace — comparable cost, weaker inventory and preorder tooling; store is secondary to the site builder.
- BigCommerce — strong customer-group gating but no advantage at this scale and no expansion story.
- Vendor-fulfilled stores (SquadLocker/1st Place/BSN) — free, zero labor, but cannot carry school-managed inventory, which is the stated objective.
- WooCommerce / custom build ($8K–20K) — the school owns security patches and maintenance with no IT staff; key-person risk.

**Why this won:** Verified best fit for every hard requirement at once — real inventory, native storewide password gating, native local pickup with automatic ready-for-pickup email, preorder support, and a huge app/labor market — at roughly $350/yr, with platform-managed uptime, security, and PCI compliance that a no-IT-staff school cannot provide itself.

**Reverse if:** The school adopts a tuition/SIS platform (e.g., FACTS) whose store module reaches feature parity, sustained volume is so low that even $350/yr exceeds fee savings versus Square's free tier, or Shopify materially reprices small plans.
