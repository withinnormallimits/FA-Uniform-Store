# 005 — Access: light gate, guest checkout, passwordless accounts, no roster sync

**Decision:** Gate the storefront with Shopify's shared password as a speed bump only, allow guest checkout, offer optional passwordless (email-code/magic-link) accounts, and do not sync the student roster into the commerce platform.

**Alternatives considered:**
- Roster-synced accounts-only store — strongest gate, but blocks new/mid-year families exactly at the August peak (top-3 edge case) and creates a FERPA/privacy exposure by pushing student data into a commerce vendor.
- Fully public store — simplest, but exposes subsidized pricing and sits poorly with a semi-private school community.
- Traditional password accounts — password resets are the single largest self-inflicted friction and support burden (Lane E).

**Why this won:** Forced accounts drive 26% of checkout abandonment and every reset is a front-office call, while roster gates fail the people with the most urgent need (new families); a leaked shared password costs little because nothing sensitive lives in the storefront — subsidies are applied off-portal (see 007) and outsiders buying spirit wear is acceptable.

**Reverse if:** Actual abuse by non-community buyers causes real harm (revisit with per-family accounts), or the store later carries items that must be entitlement-checked per family.
