# 003 — Fulfillment: free front-office pickup by default

**Decision:** Free school pickup (published days, pre-bagged by family, signature/scan at handoff) is the default; home shipping exists only at real carrier cost.

**Alternatives considered:**
- Backpack/classroom delivery — cheapest, but least auditable channel with the highest dispute rate (Lane H).
- Flat-rate or free shipping — quietly eats margin now that USPS's July 2026 repricing puts a single shirt at $5–8+ (verified).
- Pickup-only, no shipping — simplest, but strands remote/aid families and summer moves.

**Why this won:** Pickup costs nothing, matches how schools already distribute goods (Lane B), and the verified USPS repricing makes subsidized shipping a permanent margin leak; a signature/scan at handoff also creates the chargeback evidence and chain of custody the red team demanded.

**Reverse if:** Pickup volume overwhelms office capacity even with staggered windows, or a majority of families demonstrably prefer paid shipping.
